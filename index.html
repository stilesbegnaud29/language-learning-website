Where can learning French take you?

Learning a new language —French— is an exciting way to discover more about the world, your community, and your own personal growth. With so many approaches available, it's completely natural for the choices to feel a little daunting at first.

While no single path works for everyone, we've created this experiential learning illustration as a friendly guide to the rich variety of opportunities open to language learners. Depending on your interests, intention, and lifestyle, meaningful objectives and alternative routes will naturally unfold along the way.

We hope our illustration, self-assessment tools, and curated online resources will inspire you to pursue your goals, chart that progress, and confidently reach the outcomes you envision—whether that's maintaining your current French level, everyday conversation, confident professional use, or formal certification.

Language Certification is available all along this learning continuum: DELF/DALF (Europe), ACTFL OPI and WPT (U.S.), Avant STAMP (U.S.) Etc.
